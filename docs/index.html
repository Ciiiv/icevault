⚠ Duplicate Card Detected
You have multiple copies of this card. Here's how to keep them sorted physically.
🏷
Label each physical card with its IceVault ID
Find the ICV-000001 number in the card detail modal. Write or print it on a small label and stick it on the toploader, sleeve, or penny sleeve of each physical card.
📸
Each copy has its own scan and grade
Two copies of the same card can have different centering, corners, and condition. Scan each one separately so the grade reflects that specific physical card.
🔍
Find duplicates in your collection
Search the player name in your collection to see all copies side by side. The Other Copies row in each card modal links directly between duplicates.
✅
Intentional duplicates are fine
Collectors often have multiple copies of the same base card. Ice Vault flags duplicates so you can label them -- it does not prevent you from saving them.
💡 Serial numbered and graded cert cards warn if the exact same serial # or cert # is found -- those are always the same physical card. Raw cards warn on player + year + brand + parallel match.
